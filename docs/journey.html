Gandharv se Sarggek tak

Har kahani kahin na kahin se shuru hoti hai. Meri shuruaat kagaz aur kalam se hui. Main likhta gaya, din pe din, raat pe raat. 200 se zyada lyrics likhe, kuch adhure, kuch poore. Par har ek line ek beej thi. Aur jaise ek ped ke har shaakha ek doosre se judi hoti hai, waise hi mere shabd bhi ek samay ke baad ek dusre se milne lage. Tab ek soch aayi: Ye sirf lyrics kyun rahe? Kyun na inhe awaaz di jaye, beat pe utara jaye? Kyunki ye shabd sirf shabd nahi the. Ye mere struggle the, meri achievements thi, mera dard, meri khushiyaan, sab kuch inmein bas gaya tha. Har line meri soch ka ek tukda thi, aur har tukda mila kar meri kahani banata tha. Aur isi kahani se paida hua Sarggek. Sarggek sirf ek naam nahi hai. Ye ek soch hai, ek sach hai. Aur sach badalta nahi. Aaj bhi wahi hai. Is safar me tumhe pata chalega ki Sarggek kaun hai, aur kaise ek “Gandharv” ne apna asli roop paaya. Lyrics se gaane tak, kagaz se mic tak, aur ek insaan se awaaz tak, meri journey jaari hai.
